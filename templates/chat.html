How to use
1
Type your question in the chat input at the bottom of the screen.
2
Press Enter or click Send — MediBot searches the medical encyclopedia for relevant information.
3
Read the answer generated from the Gale Encyclopedia of Medicine. Always consult a doctor for personal advice.
4
Check History to review your past questions and answers from previous sessions.
